What is
Image Manipulation?

Photo manipulation has been around since the dawn of photography. There's no doubt that computerized photo modification programs like Photoshop have taken the process to a whole new level, but picture makers have been changing the ingredients in their photos for over a century. Back in the days of film, everything was done in the darkroom. These days, thanks to cutting-edge innovations, there is no limit to the changes that can be made to our photos, and we can let our imaginations fly. In case you are wondering, there is an important difference between image alteration and photo control in any case.
By changing photos, we are upgrading the data that is currently displayed in the images. This includes everything from changing the presentation and tones to resizing and trimming. Photo control, on the other hand, includes changing what's in the picture - including or reducing components, significantly changing colors or tones, and so on.
